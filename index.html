The power of automation at the service of biology

Genome Foundry is pioneering high-throughput synthetic biology services for engineering life.

As a cutting edge innovator researching novel antibiotics and other therapeutics as well as applications across food, agriculture and fragrances, what will you build and test at a scale of 1,000? 100,000?

Build more. Test more.

Our end-to-end solutions for the design, construction, testing, and analysis of a user-defined genome at a large scale are vital to address synthetic biology-related research efforts where time, cost, scalability, and result quality are of utmost importance.
