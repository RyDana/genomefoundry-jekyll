Services

We work with researchers, startups, and corporations across the globe.

Protocols can be automated, performed remotely, and results shipped to your door.
All data belongs to you and we don’t take equity or IP.
Priced to keep you cost-competitive and capital efficient.

  • Automated DNA fragment analysis, ddPCR, qPCR
